About Our College

Legacy of Excellence

New Arts, Commerce and Science College, Parner is a premier institution committed to providing quality education and fostering holistic development. Accredited with NAAC A++ Grade (3.54 CGPA), we stand as a beacon of academic excellence in the region.

Our Commitment

We offer diverse programs across Arts, Commerce, Science, and Vocational Education, preparing students for successful careers and meaningful contributions to society through innovative pedagogy and industry partnerships.

Vision & Mission

Guided by our core values and commitment to excellence in education

Our Vision

To be a leading institution of higher education that nurtures innovative thinking, promotes research excellence, and develops socially responsible global citizens committed to sustainable development and ethical values.

Our Mission

To provide quality education through innovative teaching-learning processes, foster research culture, promote ethical values, and empower students with knowledge, skills, and competencies for holistic development and lifelong learning.

About Institution

New Arts, Commerce and Science College, Parner was established with a vision to provide quality higher education to the youth of rural and semi-urban areas. Over the years, our institution has grown to become one of the premier colleges in the region, known for academic excellence and holistic development.

We are proud to be affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and have consistently maintained high academic standards. Our NAAC A++ Grade (3.54 CGPA) is a testament to our commitment to quality education.

Our History

Founded with the mission to provide accessible quality education, our college has a rich history of academic achievements and social contributions. We have been serving the community for several decades.

Achievements

NAAC A++ Grade, NIRF participation, numerous research publications, successful placement records, and recognition for excellence in various academic and co-curricular activities.

Perspective Plan

Our perspective plan focuses on continuous improvement in teaching-learning processes, infrastructure development, research promotion, and student-centric initiatives. We aim to be a center of excellence in higher education.

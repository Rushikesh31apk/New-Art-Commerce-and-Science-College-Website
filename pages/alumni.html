Maji Vidhyarthi Sangh (Alumni Association)

The Alumni Association serves as a bridge between the college and its former students, fostering lifelong connections and contributing to institutional development.

Aims & Objectives

  • Create awareness of higher education and its necessity
  • Develop reading, writing, speaking and conversation skills of students
  • Suggest and discuss academic and administrative patterns
  • Encourage students for better performance
  • Cooperate with students for academic and other activities
  • Conduct debates on burning problems and increase social awareness
  • Create awareness about nature and nature conservation
  • Guide students regarding employment and self-employment
  • Organize sports camps and cultural activities
  • Felicitate teachers for their achievements
  • Provide financial assistance to needy and poor students
  • Motivate students towards achieving their goals

Extension Activities

NSS (National Service Scheme)

NSS unit actively engages in community service, social awareness campaigns, and rural development activities. Our volunteers contribute significantly to society through various programs.

  • Blood Donation Camps
  • Literacy Programs
  • Environmental Awareness
  • Health Camps

NCC (National Cadet Corps)

NCC wing trains students in discipline, leadership, and patriotism. Cadets participate in camps, parades, and national-level activities.

  • Annual Training Camps
  • Republic Day Parade
  • Adventure Activities
  • Leadership Training

Woman Empowerment Cell

Promoting gender equality and empowering women through various programs and awareness campaigns.

Board of Student Development

Fostering overall personality development and leadership skills among students.

Health Center

Providing basic healthcare facilities and organizing health awareness programs for students.

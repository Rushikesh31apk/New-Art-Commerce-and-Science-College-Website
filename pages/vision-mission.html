Vision, Mission & Objectives

Guiding principles that drive our institution

OUR VISION

"To make social development through quality education to poor and socioeconomically deprived masses and rural youth."

OUR MISSION

"To make all round personality development of students through a disciplined teaching-learning process."

OUR OBJECTIVES

1
Student Encouragement

To encourage students in general and girls in particular.

2
Modern Techniques

To encourage students to learn modern techniques and methodologies.

3
Global Competencies

To develop the competencies among students to face global challenges.

4
Scientific Temper

To inculcate a scientific temper and a humanitarian approach among society.

5
National Development

To address global and local needs towards national development.

6
Value Inculcation

To sensitize students with a sense of belongingness, integrity, and grattitudeness.

OUR CORE VALUES

Excellence
Integrity
Social Responsibility
Innovation
